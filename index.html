01. About

About Me

I'm a Data Scientist based in Seattle, WA with a masters degree in Data Science . I'm passionate about applying machine learning to solve real problems and always with a focus on measurable business impact over theoretical complexity.

My work spans the full ML/DS lifecycle: exploratory analysis, feature engineering, model training and evaluation, and communicating results clearly to non-technical stakeholders. Each project I take on is grounded in a clear business question with concrete, quantifiable outcomes.

I've applied these skills across domains including semantic segmentation customer segmentation, causal inference, statistical testing, and deep learning-based recommendation systems. I'm currently open to Data Scientist and ML Engineer roles.
